The Best Movies According to Keely

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Little Shop of Horrors

There are very few musicals that stand out to me and make a likes, let alone favorites list. This is a classic!

New York, I love You

Any movie that chronicles a complex metropolitan city like New York wins me over. I like this movie and its display of dynamic, intertwined characters

Life Itself

Heartbreaking and heartwarming at the same time.
